Tile Puzzles

Tile Puzzles is a simple game of creating the picture out of the tiles. Place the tiles in the picture frame and if you found the correct position it will lock into place. Their is a total of 5 levels, each with 3 puzzles that range in difficulty. Each puzzle is timed, and if the time runs out you will be prompted to enter a high score. Every time you complete a puzzle your score will be scaled by how much time is remaining. Please enjoy this game and have fun!
