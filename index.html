About Me

My name is Jeremiah. I am a full stack developer with practice in frameworks, data, api's', cloud hosting, web applications, game development, and mobile applications. Pround husband and father of two amazing boy's. I have three cat's who love to program(step on my key-board), and two dog's.

Fast forward 25 years, I decided to go back to my original pasion! So I obtained my bachelors degree in Computer Information Systems, and completed a college level bootcamp for full stack devoplment.

My mission is to create what ever application you desire to work as intended with the best quality. I work hard to stay in direct contact with my stake holders to make sure acceptance criteria is met and projects can be delivered in a timely fassion, and, of course, working as expected.
